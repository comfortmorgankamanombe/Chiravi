About Our Resort

Located in the prestigious low-density suburbs of Bindura, Mashonaland Central Province, Chiravi Resorts offers a perfect blend of tranquility and accessibility.

Our property is easily reachable from major transportation routes while maintaining the peaceful, secure environment our discerning guests expect. Nestled amidst lush gardens and breathtaking landscapes, we provide an oasis of relaxation and luxury.
